A ride around El Chaten


El Chalten is the Argentine capital of trekking .
In this amazing natural scenario you will have multiple options for walking, climbing, and lot of things.
You will feel strong emotions while arriving seeing for the first time the mountains and glaciers that makes Patagonia unique.


Instagram

Trekkings that are a must:

Chorrilo del salto

Nice, short and easy walk that takes you inside the woods until you get to a beautiful waterfall of more than 20 meters of high

Laguna de los tres

The most sought-after walk to the natural viewpoint that is closest to the imposing walls of the Fitz Roy.

Laguna Torre

A valley walk that will take you to an overwhelming place of mountains and glaciers. You will see Cerro Torre, the most difficult and extraordinary climbs in the world.

Its name is due to the mythical mountain that stands out from the rest of the peaks that surround it. Chalten is a term of the Aonikenk language with which ancient Tehuelches named this smoking mountain
